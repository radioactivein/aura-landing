Leading Manufacturer & Exporter of Kerala Snacks

Aura Foods Pvt. Ltd., founded over 15 years ago, has grown from a small venture into a globally trusted brand for authentic Kerala snacks. With advanced manufacturing units in Palakkad and Pollachi, we produce over 10 tonnes of premium snacks daily, maintaining world-class standards in quality and hygiene.

We specialize in banana chips, tapioca chips, jackfruit chips, murukku, and Kerala mixtures, crafted using traditional recipes and refined export-quality oil. From sourcing ingredients directly from farmers to packaging and export, every step is handled with precision — ensuring freshness, consistency, and authentic Kerala taste in every bite.
